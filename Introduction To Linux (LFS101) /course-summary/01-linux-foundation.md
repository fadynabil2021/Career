# 01. The Linux Foundation

## Learning Objectives

* Understand the role and mission of The Linux Foundation.
* Identify major open-source projects and governing principles.
* Recognize the ecosystem that surrounds Linux: foundations, vendors, and communities.

## Key Concepts Explained

* **What is The Linux Foundation**

  * Plain-language: An organization that supports the growth of Linux and collaborative open-source projects by providing infrastructure, governance, training, and legal guidance.
  * Why it matters: It enables large-scale, vendor-neutral collaboration and helps critical projects remain sustainable and legally safe for enterprises and individuals.
  * How it works internally: Funds, membership tiers (corporates, sponsors), project governance committees, and shared services (CI, code hosting, trademarks).
